FAQ

Common questions.

Everything local business owners ask before getting started.

I already pay for SEO — how is this different? ▾
Traditional SEO gets you ranked on Google's blue-link results page. GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) is about getting named by AI tools like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overview — which now answer questions directly without showing a list of links. Both matter, but they require different signals. Your SEO investment still counts; this audit identifies the additional layer your SEO agency likely isn't covering.
Are my customers actually using ChatGPT to find a local service? ▾
More than you'd think — and it's growing fast. ChatGPT crossed 200 million weekly users in 2025. Google AI Overview now appears at the top of roughly 50% of local service searches. Voice assistants (Siri, Alexa, Google Assistant) also pull from the same AI data sources. Even if your current customers found you the old way, the next generation of customers is starting their search with a question to an AI — not a Google search.
My phone is already ringing — why should I care? ▾
If your phone is ringing, it means some customers are finding you — but it doesn't tell you how many called a competitor instead. Every time an AI recommends a competitor instead of your business, that's a job that didn't go to your calendar. The goal isn't to replace what's working — it's to close the gap on the calls you're not getting.
Do I need to hire a developer or can I do this myself? ▾
It depends on the task. Adding schema code to your website does require access to your site's HTML or CMS — your web developer can do this in under an hour using the generated code in the action plan. Everything else — updating your Google Business Profile, responding to reviews, uploading photos, fixing Bing Places — you can do yourself with no technical skills.
How long before I start showing up in AI results? ▾
Schema and GBP changes typically take 2–6 weeks to be picked up by AI crawlers. Content changes can be indexed faster — sometimes within days. The full effect of a complete AI visibility optimisation is usually visible within 60–90 days. Unlike paid ads, the results compound over time and don't stop when you stop paying.
What happens if I do nothing? ▾
The risk is gradual but real. AI tools are updated constantly, and businesses that get cited early tend to stay cited — because citations build authority which drives more citations. Competitors who act now will build a structural advantage that gets harder to close. Waiting 6 months makes catching up significantly harder.
